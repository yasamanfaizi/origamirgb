Interesting Facts About Camels

Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communtication among chameleons.
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Teddy Bears

  1. The Teddy Bears' Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear.
  2. Winnie the Pooh was based on a real bear.
  3. The smallest commercially available teddy bear is a mere 0.29 inches tall, made by South African "microbear" maker Cheryl Moss, whose largest creations are just taller than half an inch.

  3. Pandas are "lazy"- eating and sleeping makes their day.
